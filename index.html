About Us

We have been making the highest quality mechanical pencils since 1952. Manufactured in the USA our pencils come in packs of 420. Each pencil extends to 3ft long, with 1ft made up of the actual pencils, while the other part of the pencil is made up of eraser. This system ensures your biggest mistakes will be erased. The technology used inside our pencils provide a professional pencil experience.
